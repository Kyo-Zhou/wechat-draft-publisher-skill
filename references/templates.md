# Templates

Templates are the visual system applied during Markdown-to-WeChat conversion.

## What A Template Controls

- first screen
- section headings
- callout cards
- quotes
- dividers
- ending block

## Recommended Built-In Templates

- `default`
- `editorial`
- `minimal`

## Design Rule

Templates should feel native to WeChat.

Avoid:

- giant web-app cards
- too many gradients
- excessive inline highlighting
- decorative clutter

Prefer:

- clean spacing
- strong section anchors
- restrained emphasis
- readable long-form rhythm
